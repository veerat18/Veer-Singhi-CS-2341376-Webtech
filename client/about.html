Bhadohi’s Handmade Carpet Legacy

The Heritage of Bhadohi

Bhadohi, proudly known as the Carpet City of India, has earned global recognition for its exceptional handmade carpets. For centuries, this region has been the heart of India’s hand-knotted carpet industry, producing masterpieces that decorate luxury homes, hotels, and interior spaces across the world.

The origins of carpet weaving in Bhadohi trace back to the Mughal era, when Persian design traditions blended beautifully with Indian artistry. Over generations, families have preserved these weaving techniques, passing them down from father to son with pride and dedication.

Each carpet woven here is not merely a product — it is a work of art. Thousands of hand-tied knots, months of patience, and extraordinary precision come together to create timeless masterpieces that reflect culture, history, and craftsmanship.

Crafted By Skilled Hands

Every knot carries a legacy of dedication and artistry.

Our Family’s 30+ Year Journey

Our father, Mr. Bajrang Singhi, has been associated with the carpet raw material business for more than 30 years. With deep expertise in sourcing premium wool, yarns, and essential materials, he has built strong trust and relationships within the industry.

Growing up around looms, yarn textures, and design discussions, we witnessed firsthand the discipline and quality standards required to create world-class carpets. For us, carpets were never just products — they were part of our family’s identity.

His experience and knowledge have shaped our understanding of craftsmanship, durability, and premium quality materials — values that remain at the core of everything we do.

From Raw Material to Masterpiece

The Birth of Veroma Rugs

Today, as two brothers — Mohit Singhi and Veer Singhi — we are building Veroma Rugs under our father’s guidance and wisdom.

Our vision is to combine Bhadohi’s traditional craftsmanship with modern luxury aesthetics, delivering handcrafted carpets that meet international standards.

Veroma Rugs represents heritage, premium raw materials, generational trust, and a new ambition to take our family’s legacy from Bhadohi to the global market.

We believe a carpet is more than décor — it defines warmth, elegance, and character within a space.
