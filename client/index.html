About Veroma Rugs

Veroma Rugs is a family-driven carpet brand built under the guidance of Mr. Bajrang Singhi, who has over 30 years of experience in the carpet raw material industry.

As brothers Mohit Singhi and Veer Singhi, we are carrying forward Bhadohi’s handmade carpet legacy with a vision to blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary luxury.
